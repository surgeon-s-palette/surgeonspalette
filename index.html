The Future of
Surgical Tool Organization

The Problem

urgical instrument tables are disorganized.

Why this happens

  • Personnel-dependent expertise
  • Different surgeons prefer different tools
  • 200+ handoffs per surgery
  • Current products not designed for organization

What this causes

  • Slower OR setup
  • Slower operations
  • Increased patient risk

For every 15 minutes of surgery time:

13% increase in risk of
Surgical Site Infections (SSI) 1, 2

$15k-$30k per SSI

Additional ~$930 in operating room costs 3

Our Product

Surgeon’s Palette is an organizational template for surgical instruments.

Key Features

  • Customizable to surgeon- and case-specific needs
    • Clients can choose which tools they want placed where on the tray in the online custom order form
    • Product is then manufactured and shipped to them
  • Speeds instrument table setup
  • Increases efficiency of tracking tools
  • Reduces time between surgeries (turnover time)
